## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

**Q: What is the one-time setup fee for subscriptions?**
A: The setup fee covers the initial development and deployment of your project. It's a one-time payment made at the start, and then you only pay the monthly subscription for ongoing maintenance, hosting, and support.

**Q: Can I switch from subscription to one-time ownership?**
A: Yes! If you're on a subscription and want to switch to one-time ownership, you can pay the remaining project cost minus the subscription payments made (up to 50% of project cost). Contact us for a custom calculation.

**Q: What's included in the monthly subscription?**
A: Subscription includes hosting, SSL, security updates, bug fixes, content updates (based on plan), performance monitoring, regular backups, and technical support. Major new features or redesigns are quoted separately.

**Q: What happens if I cancel my subscription?**
A: You can cancel anytime with 30 days notice. We'll provide all source files and help migrate to your own hosting if needed.

**Q: Do you offer discounts?**
A: Yes! We offer up to 20% discount for registered startups and non-profit organizations. For annual subscriptions, contact us for special pricing.
