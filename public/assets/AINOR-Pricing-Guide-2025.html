Frequently Asked Questions

What is the one-time setup fee for subscriptions?
The setup fee covers the initial development and deployment of your project. It's a one-time payment made at the start, and then you only pay the monthly subscription for ongoing maintenance, hosting, and support.
Can I switch from subscription to one-time ownership?
Yes! If you're on a subscription and want to switch to one-time ownership, you can pay the remaining project cost minus the subscription payments made (up to 50% of project cost). Contact us for a custom calculation.
What's included in the monthly subscription?
Subscription includes hosting, SSL, security updates, bug fixes, content updates (based on plan), performance monitoring, regular backups, and technical support. Major new features or redesigns are quoted separately.
What happens if I cancel my subscription?
You can cancel anytime with 30 days notice. We'll provide all source files and help migrate to your own hosting if needed. Your website/app will continue to work, but you'll need to arrange your own hosting and maintenance.
Do you offer discounts?
Yes! We offer up to 20% discount for registered startups and non-profit organizations. For annual subscriptions, contact us for special pricing. We also offer custom enterprise packages.
